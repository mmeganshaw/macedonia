YOUR PREMIER LASWERWASH

Welcome to Macedonia Laser Wash. We are a family owned and operated business that is pleased to provide our customers with a completely touchless car wash.

Our bays are equipped with LaserWash Smart 360 systems that use lasers to measure your vehicle and tailor each wash to its exact dimensions and position in the bay. We also use only hot, softened water, including a final, spot-free rinse cycle with water that has been purified using reverse osmosis filtration (an RO system), which removes minerals known to cause spotting. Rest easy knowing that nothing is touching your vehicle except cleaning products and water, and enjoy an exceptionally clean, damage-free car!
